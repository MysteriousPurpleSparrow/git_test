Why does everyone think they're really smart?

Programming can really make people feel smart for knowing even the most basic tasks. Do you ever wonder why that is? I can think of a few reasons why that might be.

Everyone's illiterate

My sister often makes jokes about how she doesn't know what a file is. This is an exageration, of course, but it illustrates a real problem. Most people don't know the first thing about their devices, so when they learn something as simple as the existance of a command line, they suddenly feel like geniuses. No one else knows what a terminal is, let alone how to open one! Look how smart I am!
